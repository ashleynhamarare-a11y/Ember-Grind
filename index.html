Our Story

Born from a
love of the craft

Ember & Grind began in a small Cape Town kitchen — just a hand grinder, a good bag of Ethiopian beans, and an obsession with getting it right.

Today we partner directly with farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, bringing you coffees with genuine terroir: bright floral notes, deep chocolate finishes, and everything in between.

Every cup is brewed to order. No shortcuts, no compromises.
